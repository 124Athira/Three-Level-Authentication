Why SafeVault?

🔐 About SafeVault Welcome to SafeVault – your trusted solution for secure and fraud-free banking transactions! 🚀 In today’s digital era, financial fraud is a growing concern, and ensuring transaction security is more important than ever. SafeVault is a machine learning-powered fraud detection system designed to analyze transactions and prevent fraudulent activities before they cause harm. Our system leverages advanced algorithms, multi-level authentication, and real-time monitoring to safeguard users from fraudsters. 🛡️💳


🌟 Key Features of SafeVault
✅AI-Based Fraud Detection 🤖 – Uses Random Forest algorithm to detect fraud.
✅Secure Transaction Processing 🔐 – Supports Credit Card and Debit Card Transactions.
✅Multi-Level Authentication 🛡️ – Three layers of security:
       🔏 Password Protection
       📩 OTP Verification via Email
       🖼️ Image-Based Security Check
✅Admin Control Panel 👨‍💻 – Manage users, transactions, and fraud detection settings.
✅User-Friendly Interface 📱 – Simple, efficient, and easy to use.


🛠️ How SafeVault Works?
🔹 Users register with their email and phone number.
🔹 They upload transaction files 📂 (Credit or Debit).
🔹 SafeVault analyzes transactions using PCA 📊 and predicts if they are valid or fraudulent.
🔹 If fraud is detected, users and admins receive instant alerts.
🔹 Users can review, verify, or report suspicious transactions.


With SafeVault, your transactions are always secure, fast, and protected! 💙🔐😊
